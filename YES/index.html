The King

King Von is a rapper and songwriter. He is a very popular rapper especially in Chicago. Not everyone in Chicago supports him though. King Von is from O'Block which is in Chicago on 64th street. It's known that there is a deadly rivalry between people from 63rd and 64th going back to the 1990's.King Von has come out with 3 albums and various singles. All 3 of his albums came with at least 13 songs. 2 albums came out in 2020 and the first one came out in 2019. His last album called "Welcome to O'Block" came out October 30th, 2020. It came out a week before King Von's tragic death. After his death millions were saddened and heartbroken. King Von's manager then came out to tell the story of what really happened and said that King Von has around 300 unreleased songs. His legacy will live on through his fans and music.

Dayvon Bennett

King Von who was born Dayvon Bennett in a neighborhood called O'Block on August 9th,1994. O'Block is actually called Parkway Garden Homes. It was renamed by the people living there after the murder of Odee Perry. Dayvon Bennett had 6 half siblings from his father and 3 siblings from his mother. His Father was frequently in and out of jail because of his crimes. Sadly, his father was shot and killed when Dayvon was just 11.

At the age of 16 Dayvon went to jail for the first time. That was only one of his many times getting into legal trouble. In 2014 he was accused of killing one person and wounding another which later on he would be acquitted after a trial by the jury. After some time he started rapping and made singles with another artist from O'Block named Lil Durk. Lil Durk would then eventually sign him to OTF (Only The Family). entertainment label.
