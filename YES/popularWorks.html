Welcome to O'Block

King Von has 3 albums but we're only going to be talking about his 2 most popular albums. One of his 2 most popular albums is called "Welcome to O'Block". His album "Welcome to O'Block" was his most popular one which came out on October 30th, 2020. The album peaked at number 5 on Billboards top 200. It was on the chart for 28 weeks. As of right now it has about 64 million streams.

Levon James

King Von's 2nd most popular album is called "Levon James". This album came out on March 6th, 2020. On Billboards top 200 it peaked at number 40. It was on the charts for 18 weeks only 10 weeks less than "Welcome to O'Block". As of right now this album has a total of 53 million streams.
